Hmmm...I smell something

by Sorcen

What happens when you are using the internet? Well in a nutshell you are constantly in a state of sending and receiving data to the web servers. This is what wllows you to communciate with the browser to click on things or send messages. The issue here is that these "data packets" are literally sent across your network to the receiving servers. With that being said what would happen if someone wanted to know exactly what you were doing on your computer? What if they were able to look at your data packets as you are sending/receiving them? Well...this is something called a packet sniffer attack or a "man in the middle" attack. It's actually pretty interesting, let's dive in.

We are going to be using a program called Wireshark in our Kali machine for todays example.
